Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic South Asian dish that features tender pieces of chicken, basmati rice, and a blend of fragrant spices.The chicken is typically marinated in yogurt, turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala, and other spices, which infuses it with deep flavors. The rice is usually cooked separately or layered with the marinated chicken and then cooked together, allowing the spices to meld perfectly.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Basmati Rice
  • Ghee
  • Onion
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Curd
  • Salt
  • Raw Spices like cardomom, clove, cinnamon, bay leaf
  • Powdered spices like Haldi, Chilli, coriander

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken for about 2 hours
  2. Prepare the rice until it is 70 percent cooked
  3. Fry the onions
  4. Partially cook the marinated chicken
  5. Layer the rice, chicken and fried onions in a pot
  6. Cook on low heat and then serve
