Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish consisting of a flat, round base of dough topped with various ingredients such as tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings, then baked to perfection. Itâ€™s a versatile dish enjoyed worldwide, with countless regional variations and customization options.

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sliced vegetables
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough by combining water, oil, flour, and salt.
  2. Roll out the dough and bake it in the oven to make the pizza base.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the base and sprinkle a generous amount of cheese on top.
  4. Arrange sliced vegetables over the pizza and bake until fully cooked.
