Maggie

Description

Maggie is an instant noodle which is very popular in India because of its affordability and quick preparation (usually 2 minutes). Maggi is often customized with vegetables, spices, and sauces, making it a comforting snack or meal.

Ingredients

  • Maggie
  • Onion
  • Ghee
  • Green Chilli
  • One extra maggie masala
  • Regular spices

Steps

  1. Put some ghee in the pan
  2. Add onion and green chilli and saute it extra maggie masala
  3. Add one cup of water and maggie then cook it for sometime
  4. Add the maggie masala that comes with the packet in half cup of boiling water
  5. Finish the cooked maggie with the above cup of water
